Orbitter Spacecrafts

What are they?

Spacecrafts that are launched and set in orbit around the Earth or other planets.

What are the diffrent types?

There are two main types:

  • Earth Satellites
  • Planetary Orbitter

Purpose:

The orbitter Spacecrafts have many utilities and functions based around collecting scientific data, navigation, reconnaissance, communications, etc.

Examples:

International Space Station
2001 Mars Odyssey
