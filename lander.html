Lander Spacecrafts

What are they?

Lander spacecraft are designed to land on the surface of another planet or other celestial body.

Purpose:

Gather planetary data about the atmosphere patterns, surface, composition during descent and after landing.

Types:

There are two types of Lander Spacecrafts:

  • Impact Rovers: high velocity impact, where the spacecraft obtains data during its descent, but is not designed to survive the impact
  • Stationary Lander or Surface Rover: “soft” landing, where the spacecraft survives and is able to perform other functions on the surface.
